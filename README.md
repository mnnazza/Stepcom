## Overview

Stepcom is an AI productivity companion designed for Gen Z users who struggle with procrastination, emotional overwhelm, and difficulty starting tasks. Unlike traditional productivity apps filled with dashboards and manual tracking, Stepcom works entirely through **natural conversation**.

The goal is simple: make progress feel easy, human, and achievable.

## Problem

Modern productivity struggles are driven by:
- Emotional overwhelm and mental fatigue  
- Chronic overthinking and digital distraction  
- Systems that require discipline before discipline is built  

Traditional tools often increase guilt and stress instead of helping users begin.

## Solution

Stepcom listens, remembers, and guides users through chat. It automatically:
- Detects emotional states and habits  
- Provides grounding and gentle motivation  
- Breaks tasks into tiny, doable micro-steps  
- Remembers goals and follows up naturally
